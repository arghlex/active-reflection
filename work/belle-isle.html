The Belle Isle

From its inception we worked with the Belle Isle on their brand identity and also developed concept artwork for their second restaurant, in Chichester. Here you can see examples of some of the projects which have been completed for them including artwork for the "SCAN&GO" discount card, typographic design for the many blackboards menus strongly inspired by 1920s/1930s type, and a 'faux-heinz' logo developed for companies very own brand of Baked beans. There were also simple black & white labels designed for flavoured syrup bottles and a wine bottle swing-ticket which was attached to every bottle on The Belle Isles wine rack.
