Theme My Screen

Theme My Screen is a one-stop shop for themed wallpaper on Mac devices. We worked with the creators, who had already established their online presence with the Theme My Screen website. They needed our help to extend their service on to the iOS market as an iPhone app. With the app, users can browse a full collection of unique wallpaper and background themes. The themes are available for iPhone 4, iPhone 5 and iPad as well.
