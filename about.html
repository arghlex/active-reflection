Hello, we are a graphic design consultancy based on the South Coast.

Our work includes: Identity & Branding, Marketing Materials and Digital Design.

Ethos

At Active Reflection we combine a technical knowledge of graphic design, printing processes and digital technology with research,open dialogue and workshops to ensure the most effective resolutions for our clients.

Multi Step Process

Every project we undertake is considered on its individual merits, however we generally operate a multi step process with fees payable at regular intervals.This ensures transparency for our clients at every stage and enables them to see exactly what they are paying for as the project progresses.

Research

A fundamental understanding of your business is paramount to how we work. In orderto achieve this we conduct research, maintain an open dialogue and present workshops to gain insight into your company, its values, strengths and ethos. We also study your competitors to understand your market position and gain insight into any marketplace conversations that are happening. We then asses the public perception of your brand, marketing materials or digital platform and how it may be improved or altered to build awareness and extend customer loyalty. We help to write the brief with you.

Action

Once we have a achieved a solid understanding of your business and its requirements, we can begin to piece together a visual strategy which will facilitate the required outcomes. We will explore numerous visual solutions and work closely with you at each stage paying attention to the overall aesthetic, typographic detail, copy text and functionality of the brief.

Solution

Once a suitable solution has been selected, we perfect, create and deliver the assets after rigorous user testing and evaluation.
